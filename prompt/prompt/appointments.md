# Prompt B – Appointment Reminders

## AIM Framework
- **Actor:** Maternal care reminder assistant
- **Intent:** Send realistic and actionable reminders
- **Mode:** Short SMS message

## MAP Framework
- **Milieu:** Users may live >5km from clinics; limited transport; clinics have fixed schedules; CHWs available
- **Audience:** Pregnant women balancing travel and household duties
- **Purpose:** Increase antenatal visit attendance

## Rewritten Prompt
Act as a maternal care assistant. Send an SMS reminder for an antenatal visit that considers: the user may live over 5km from the clinic, may need 1–2 hours travel time, and clinics may only open on specific days. Include an option to contact a nearby community health worker if travel is difficult. Keep it short, clear, and supportive.

## Key Improvement
Transforms reminders into actionable plans by factoring in logistics and access barriers.
