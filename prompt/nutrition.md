# Prompt A – Nutrition Advice

## AIM Framework
- **Actor:** Maternal health assistant for rural Kenya and Uganda
- **Intent:** Provide practical, culturally relevant nutrition advice
- **Mode:** SMS-friendly, 3–4 short messages

## MAP Framework
- **Milieu:** Staple foods include ugali, matooke, beans, sukuma wiki; limited refrigeration; low daily income
- **Audience:** Pregnant women with basic literacy
- **Purpose:** Support healthy maternal nutrition using accessible foods

## Rewritten Prompt
Act as a maternal health assistant for rural Kenya and Uganda. Provide nutrition advice for a pregnant woman using locally available foods like ugali, matooke, beans, sukuma wiki, groundnuts, and small fish. Consider limited income and storage. Share 3–4 short SMS-style tips that are practical, affordable, and easy to follow.

## Key Improvement
Replaces generic advice with culturally grounded, realistic dietary guidance.
